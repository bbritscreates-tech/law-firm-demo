ABOUT US

Established in 1995, Harrington & Cole Attorneys has built a reputation for delivering professional and reliable legal services across Cape Town and the surrounding areas. With our main office in the heart of the city and plans for expansion, we aim to provide accessible and high-quality legal support to both businesses and private clients throughout the region.

Our firm specializes in corporate and commercial law, property transactions, debt recovery, and civil litigation. We take pride in offering both personalized attention and efficient, results-driven service, ensuring that our clients receive expert guidance tailored to their unique needs. Whether working with individual clients or larger institutions, our commitment to excellence and integrity underpins every matter we handle.

Operating across multiple jurisdictions, including local magistrates’ courts and the regional high court, Harrington & Cole Attorneys is well-equipped to manage a diverse range of legal matters. Our team also maintains strong ties with key property and financial institutions, allowing us to provide informed and strategic advice to support both commercial and private interests.

We embrace modern technology to streamline our workflows and maximize efficiency, allowing us to handle complex legal processes with accuracy and speed. This commitment to innovation ensures that our services remain practical, cost-effective, and client-focused.

At Harrington & Cole Attorneys, our goal is simple: to provide clear, reliable, and professional legal solutions while maintaining a friendly and approachable service experience for all our clients.
